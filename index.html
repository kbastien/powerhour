Power Hour

Welcome to the best Power Hour site you have ever seen. Here you'll be able to Power Hour with your friends, watching a bunch of mostly Hip-Hop videos.

  1. Fill up a shot glass full of whatever liquid you desire.
  2. After every minute, the video will change and you will hear a horn. Then take a shot of your drink.
  3. After 60 minutes, the Power Hour is over and you can move on with your life.
